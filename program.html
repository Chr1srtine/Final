Program

1:1 Private Lessons

For a deeper and more personalized learning experience in violin and music, we offer one-on-one private lessons tailored to each student’s goals and pace. To support effective practice and review, students or their guardians are welcome to record lessons for personal use outside of class.

Student Recitals

We host student recitals once every few months to provide valuable stage experience. Performance opportunities are essential for musicians of all levels, helping them develop confidence, stage presence, and professional concert etiquette. Students also learn how to adjust their sound to suit different acoustic environments, creating a more resonant and expressive performance. In addition, we guide students on how to manage nerves and perform confidently under pressure, building both musical and mental resilience.

Basics and Sight Reading

We do not aim simply to play pieces. Our focus is on thoroughly mastering the fundamentals; intonation, tone, rhythm, and more, and applying these skills thoughtfully to each piece, creating more polished and expressive performances. In addition, students learn how to convey emotions musically, and through sight-reading practice, they develop the ability to quickly grasp a piece’s rhythm, structure, and pitches, significantly enhancing their overall musical understanding.

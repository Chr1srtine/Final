Christine's violin studio is based in South Korea and in Cincinnati, Ohio in the United States. We offer violin lessons to students of all ages and levels from beginners to advanced. Please feel free to fill out a form on our “contact” page for more information or scheduling a trial lesson.

Musical Vision

"Music does not only express or create emotion, but it is also a powerful tool that records and brings out the memory in our lives. My hope as a musician is to recall someone’s memory and enrich their lives with my music."
